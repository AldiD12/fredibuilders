### ‚úÖ Strengths:

- Clear, confident answers
- Mentions 12-month guarantee

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Missing Key Objections:
- "Do I need to move my furniture?"
- "Will there be dust everywhere?"
- "What if I find a defect after you leave?"
- "How do you protect my expensive flooring?"
- "Can I stay in my home during the work?"

### Recommended Additional FAQs:

**Q: "Will there be dust everywhere?"**
A: "We use industrial-grade dust extraction systems and seal off work areas with heavy-duty plastic sheeting. While some dust is inevitable in renovation work, we minimize it to levels that won't affect the rest of your home. We also vacuum and clean at the end of each day."

**Q: "Can I stay in my home during the work?"**
A: "Yes. Most of our clients remain in their homes during bathroom renovations. We work in contained areas, maintain access to other facilities where possible, and keep disruption to an absolute minimum. For full-house projects, we can discuss phased approaches."

**Q: "How do you protect expensive flooring and furniture?"**
A: "We treat your home with the same care we'd treat our own. All floors are covered with heavy-duty protective sheeting, furniture is wrapped and moved carefully, and we use corner protectors when moving materials through your home."
